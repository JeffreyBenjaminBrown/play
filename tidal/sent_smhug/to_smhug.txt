Tidal is *easy*. No monad or music understanding needed.

Source
======
The "stable" branch of Tidal, on Hackage:  
  https://hackage.haskell.org/package/tidal
The "devel" branch (I have found no problems in it): 
  https://github.com/tidalcycles/Tidal

Install
=======
Installation guide:
    http://tidal.lurk.org/getting_started.html
Linux there's an install script for Linux. I used it; piece of cake. (Jack was a bit of a struggle, but out of scope.)
    https://raw.githubusercontent.com/tidalcycles/Tidal/master/doc/install-linux.sh
